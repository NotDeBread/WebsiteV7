THE SERVER 'CST Server', made back in December of 2024, was originally a small project of mine made for my Computer Servicing class. Season One Season one is currently the longest standing version in the servers history, also being the most successfull (And my personal favorite). Several large scale buildings were made (mostly by TheTrueSkywalkr) and many different evens occured. We had around 8 concurrent players cauing the server to lag immensely, drama started to unfold immediately, and the world generation was awful.
